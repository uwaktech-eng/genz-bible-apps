Genz EduTech Innovation • Offline KJV Bible • Android + PC slots

Read the King James Bible offline and switch to other public-domain Bible versions with a soft, premium, glassy experience by Genz EduTech Innovation.

Users can register after payment, wait for admin approval, then unlock the app on exactly one Android device and one PC — the first ones they sign in with.

Installable PWA
Host on GitHub + Vercel, then install on Android, tablet, or desktop.
Setup tip: Replace the placeholder values in window.APP_CONFIG with your Selar link, Apps Script Web App /exec URL, brand contact details, and your Google Sheet ID in Code.gs.
Device rule: one Android and one PC only. The first Android and first PC used for login become the allowed devices for that account until an admin resets them.

Users pay first on Selar, then submit their email and payment reference here for admin approval.

Terms & Conditions

  • Access is granted only to approved users after payment verification.
  • Each approved user is limited to one Android device and one PC, being the first of each type used to sign in.
  • Sharing login details, sharing paid access, or attempting to bypass device limits may lead to suspension.
  • Payment disputes, resets, and account status updates are handled by the administrators of Genz EduTech Innovation.

Privacy Policy

  • This app stores account, approval, and device-slot data in Google Sheets through Google Apps Script.
  • Bookmarks and local device IDs are stored on the user’s device for app functionality.
  • Only the data required to approve access, manage accounts, and enforce device limits is collected.
  • Users can contact Genz EduTech Innovation to request password resets or account assistance.
