Quick usage tips

  • Drag inside the graph to pan across the plane.
  • Use the mouse wheel or toolbar to zoom in and out smoothly.
  • Tap “Trace” on a function to follow its coordinates on hover.
  • Enter domain limits to show only part of a graph.
  • Use “Analyze selected graph” for roots, turning points, and area estimates.
